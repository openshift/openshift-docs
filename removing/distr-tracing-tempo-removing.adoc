= Removing the {dt}
:context: distr-tracing-tempo-removing

toc::[]

The steps for removing the {dt} from an {product-title} cluster are as follows:

. Shut down all the {dt} pods.
. Remove any TempoStack instances.
. Remove the {TempoOperator}.
