= Evaluate autonomous agentic troubleshooting

[role="_abstract"]
Autonomous agentic troubleshooting is an opt-in feature of {ols-official} that uses an AI agent to monitor cluster alerts, diagnose issues, and propose remediation actions with human approval at every critical step. Evaluate the feature's capabilities, action scope, and limitations to determine whether it meets your cluster management requirements.
