The first step in troubleshooting is to retrieve the logs and pods status.
The logs allow you to identify the root cause of an error.
In addition, examining the pod's most recent status can provide information about failure messages.

=== Retrieving logs from a RHACS diagnostic bundle

You can also access Collector logs by downloading a diagnostic bundle from the {product-title} ({product-title-short}) user interface. Once you have downloaded the diagnostic bundle, you can inspect the logs for all the Collector pods. For more information, see xref:../configuration/generate-diagnostic-bundle.adoc[Generating a diagnostic bundle].
