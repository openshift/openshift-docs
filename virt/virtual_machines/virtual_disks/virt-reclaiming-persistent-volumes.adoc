= Reclaiming persistent volumes
include::modules/virt-document-attributes.adoc[]
:context: virt-reclaiming-persistent-volumes
toc::[]

When a statically-provisioned persistent volume is unbound from a
persistent volume claim, it's status is *released*.

However, before a persistent volume can be bound to a new persistent volume
claim, the volume must be _reclaimed_.
