= Introduction to {prodname}
:context: rhcl-introduction

//toc:[]

[role="_abstract"]
As a platform engineers or application developers, you can use {prodname-full} as a single data plane to connect, protect and manage your services and infrastructure.

Extending the benefits of Gateway API, {prodname} attaches policies to gateways, enabling features such as DNS connectivity, automatic TLS integration, authorization, and rate limiting for ingress traffic. This Kubernetes-native solution ensures traffic management and policy enforcement in a single cluster or across multiple clusters.
