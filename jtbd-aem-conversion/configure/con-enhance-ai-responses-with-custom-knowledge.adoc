= Enhance responses with your documentation

[role="_abstract"]
Extend the knowledge available to {ols-official} by providing your own documentation and configuring the Offline Knowledge Portal. Custom documentation supplements the default {ocp-product-title} documentation that the AI uses to generate responses.
