= Integrate OpenShift Lightspeed with Red Hat OpenShift AI

[role="_abstract"]
As an administrator, you can integrate Red Hat OpenShift AI as a large language model (LLM) provider for {ols-long}. Create a credential secret with your OpenShift AI credentials, configure the `OLSConfig` custom resource, and verify the deployment.
