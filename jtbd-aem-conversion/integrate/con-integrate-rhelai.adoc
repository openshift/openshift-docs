= Integrate OpenShift Lightspeed with Red Hat Enterprise Linux AI

[role="_abstract"]
As an administrator, you can integrate Red Hat Enterprise Linux AI (RHELAI) as a large language model (LLM) provider for {ols-long}. Create a credential secret with your RHELAI credentials, configure the `OLSConfig` custom resource, and verify the deployment.
