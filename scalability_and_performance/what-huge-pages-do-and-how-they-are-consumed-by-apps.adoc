= What huge pages do and how they are consumed by applications
include::_attributes/common-attributes.adoc[]
:context: huge-pages

toc::[]

[role="_abstract"]
Huge pages are a memory management feature that uses larger memory pages than the standard 4 Ki size to reduce translation lookaside buffer (TLB) misses and improve application performance. You can configure and consume huge pages in your {product-title} cluster.
