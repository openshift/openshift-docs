= Operators overview
include::_attributes/common-attributes.adoc[]
:context: operators-overview

toc::[]

[role="_abstract"]
Operators are the foundational control plane extensions of {product-title}. They are the preferred method of packaging, deploying, and managing services on the control plane. They can also provide advantages to applications that users run.

Operators integrate with Kubernetes APIs and CLI tools such as `kubectl` and the {oc-first}. They provide the means of monitoring applications, performing health checks, managing over-the-air (OTA) updates, and ensuring that applications remain in your specified state.
