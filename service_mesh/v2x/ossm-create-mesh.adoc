= Adding workloads to a service mesh
include::modules/ossm-document-attributes.adoc[]

After installing the operators and `ServiceMeshControlPlane` resource, create a `ServiceMeshMemberRoll` resource and specify the namespaces that you want to add to your mesh. In the `ServiceMeshMemberRoll` resource make up the data plane of your service mesh and are the applications and workflows that are controlled by the `ServiceMeshControlPlane` resource.
