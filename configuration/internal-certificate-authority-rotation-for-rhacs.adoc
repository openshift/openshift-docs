= Internal certificate authority rotation for RHACS
include::modules/common-attributes.adoc[]
:context: internal-certificate-authority-rotation-for-rhacs

toc::[]

[role="_abstract"]

The following points explain why certificate authority (CA) rotation is important in {rh-rhacs-first}:

* {product-title-short} includes an internal CA with a 5-year validity. Central and secured cluster service certificates depend on this CA. If the CA expires, {product-title-short} stops working.
* Previously, if the internal CA expired, you had to delete the `central-tls` secret and manually re-register all the secured clusters. Starting with {product-title-short} 4.9, the Operator manages CA rotation. Central simultaneously trusts two CAs, enabling overlap and a smoother migration.
