include::modules/common-attributes.adoc[]
:context: integrate-with-amazon-s3

[role="_abstract"]
You can integrate {product-title} with Amazon S3 to enable data backups. You can use these backups for data restoration in the case of an infrastructure disaster or corrupt data. After you integrate with Amazon S3, you can schedule daily or weekly backups and perform manual on-demand backups.
