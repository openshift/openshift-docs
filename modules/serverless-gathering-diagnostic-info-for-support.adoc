= Collecting diagnostic information for support

[role="_abstract"]
When you open a support case, share debugging information about your cluster with Red Hat Support. You can use the `must-gather` tool to collect diagnostic information about your {ocp-product-title} cluster, including data related to {ServerlessProductName}. For faster support, give diagnostic information for both {ocp-product-title} and {ServerlessProductName}.
