= Deletion policy

Red{nbsp}Hat reserves the right to delete {product-title} clusters after 30 days have passed with no response to service notications sent requiring actions, including but not limited to:

* Actions to restore the health of the cluster.
* Actions to restore cloud resources such as Identity and Access Management (IAM) roles, policies, and OIDC providers.
* Such deletion may leave cloud resources in the AWS account, incurring cloud costs.
* Any applications or business hosted on the cluster will be impacted.
