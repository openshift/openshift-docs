= Reviewing and approving a remediation

[role="_abstract"]
Review the agent's diagnosis and approve or reject the proposed remediation actions from the Agentic Runs view.

.Prerequisites

* You are logged in to the {ocp-product-title} web console as a cluster administrator.
* An agentic run is in the *Proposed* status.

.Procedure

. In the *Agentic Runs* view, click a run with a *Proposed* status.

. Review the diagnosis details, including:
+
* The root cause analysis
* The risk assessment
* The exact remediation actions the agent proposes to execute

. Choose one of the following actions:
+
--
* Click *Review Details* to examine the full diagnostic artifacts and remediation steps before making a decision.
* Click *Approve* to approve the proposed remediation for execution. A confirmation dialog appears before execution begins.
* Click *Reject* or *Deny* to reject the proposed remediation. The run is closed without executing any changes.
--

. If you clicked *Approve*, confirm your decision in the *Execute Remediation* confirmation step. The agent begins executing the approved remediation in a sandboxed pod.
