= Using your {companyname} subscription

[role="_abstract"]
{companyname} {prodname} is provided through a software subscription. To manage your subscriptions, access your account at the {companyname} Customer Portal.

[id="rhcl-managing_your_subscription_{context}"]
== Managing your subscriptions

. Go to link:https://access.redhat.com[access.redhat.com^].
. If you do not already have an account, create one.
. Log in to your account.
. In the menu bar, click *Subscriptions* to view and manage your subscriptions.

////
[discrete]
== Downloading zip and tar files

To access `.zip` or `.tar` files, use the Customer Portal to find the relevant files for download.
If you are using RPM packages, this step is not required.

. Open a browser and log in to the {companyname} Customer Portal *Product
  Downloads* page at link:https://access.redhat.com/downloads[access.redhat.com/downloads^].

. Scroll down to *INTEGRATION AND AUTOMATION*.

. Click *{companyname} {prodname}* to display the {prodname} downloads page.

. Click the *Download* link for your component.
