= Using your {rh} subscription

[role="_abstract"]
{rh} {prodname} is provided through a software subscription. To manage your subscriptions, access your account at the {rh} Customer Portal.

[id="rhcl-managing_your_subscription_{context}"]
== Managing your subscriptions

. Go to link:https://access.redhat.com[access.redhat.com^].
. If you do not already have an account, create one.
. Log in to your account.
. In the menu bar, click *Subscriptions* to view and manage your subscriptions.
