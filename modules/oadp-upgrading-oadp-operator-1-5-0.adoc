= Upgrading the OADP Operator

Use the following procedure when upgrading the {oadp-first} Operator.

[NOTE]
====
Do not install OADP 1.5.0 on a Openshift 4.18 cluster.
====

.Prerequisites

. You have installed the latest {oadp-short} {oadp-version-1-4}.
. You have backed up your data.

.Procedure

. Upgrade {OCP-short} 4.18 to {OCP-short} 4.19.
+
[NOTE]
====
The {oadp-first} 1.4 is not supported on {OCP-short} 4.19.
====
. Change your subscription channel for the OADP Operator from `stable-1.4` to `stable`.
. Wait for the Operator and containers to update and restart.
