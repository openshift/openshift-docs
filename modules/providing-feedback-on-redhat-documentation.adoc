= Providing feedback on {rh} documentation

{rh} appreciates your feedback on product documentation.

To propose improvements, open a Jira issue and describe your suggested changes.
Provide as much detail as possible to help the documentation team to address your request quickly.

.Prerequisite

* You have a {rh} Customer Portal account.
This account enables you to log in to the {rh} Jira Software instance.
If you do not have an account, you will be prompted to create one.

.Procedure

. Click the following link: https://issues.redhat.com/secure/CreateIssueDetails!init.jspa?pid=12342724&issuetype=1&components=12401550&priority=3&description=URL%20where%20issue%20was%20found%3A%C2%A0%0A%0ADescription%20of%20issue%3A%C2%A0&12368953[Create issue].

. In the *Summary* text box, enter a brief description of the issue.

. In the *Description* text box, provide the following information:

* The URL of the page where you found the issue.
* A detailed description of the issue. You can leave the information in other fields at their default values.

. In the *Reporter* field, enter your Jira user name.

. Click *Create* to submit the Jira issue to the documentation team.

Thank you for taking the time to provide feedback.
