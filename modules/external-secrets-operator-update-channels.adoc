= Understanding update channels of the {external-secrets-operator}

[role="_abstract"]
Control the version of the {external-secrets-operator} in your cluster by selecting an update channel. This mechanism allows you to declare a specific version track, helping ensure your environment receives only the updates you require for stability.

The {external-secrets-operator} offers the following update channels:

* `stable-v1`
* `stable-v1.y`
