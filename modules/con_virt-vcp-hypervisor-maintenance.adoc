= Hosting cluster maintenance for virtualized control planes

[role="_abstract"]
When you perform maintenance on a hosting cluster node, such as applying operating system updates or hardware repairs, follow these principles to avoid disrupting the control plane VMs in the target cluster:

* Verify that all control plane VMs are healthy and `etcd` quorum is intact before cordoning any node.
* Drain one hosting cluster node at a time.
* After the drained node's control plane VM is shut down, confirm that the remaining control plane VMs maintain `etcd` quorum before proceeding with maintenance.
* Return the node to service and verify that the VM and `etcd` member are healthy before moving to the next node.
