= Readiness probes for virtualized control planes

[role="_abstract"]
Configure readiness probes on the control plane VMs to verify that the VM operating system and critical services are operational.
The hosting cluster uses readiness probe results to determine whether a VM is healthy before allowing operations that affect other VMs in the same target cluster.

Readiness probes work together with pod disruption budgets to ensure that the hosting cluster does not proceed with draining a node until the previously affected control plane VM is confirmed healthy on another node.
