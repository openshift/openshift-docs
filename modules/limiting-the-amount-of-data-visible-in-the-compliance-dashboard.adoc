= Limiting the amount of data visible in the compliance dashboard

[role="_abstract"]
You can filter compliance data by clusters, industry standards, and control status to limit the amount of data visible in the compliance dashboard.

By filtering the compliance data, you can focus your attention on a subset of clusters, industry standards, or passed or failed controls.

.Procedure

. In the {product-title-short} portal, click *Compliance -> Dashboard*.
. Click either the *clusters*,  *namespaces*, or *nodes* tab to open the details page.
. Enter your filtering criteria in the search bar, and then click *Enter*.
