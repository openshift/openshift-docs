= Diagnostic bundle overview

[role="_abstract"]
Red{nbsp}Hat might request that you send the diagnostic bundle during investigation of your issues with {product-title}.

You can generate a diagnostic bundle and inspect its data before sending.

[NOTE]
====
The diagnostic bundle does not use encryption, and depending upon the number of clusters in your environment, the bundle size is between 100 KB and 1 MB.
Always use an encrypted channel to transfer this data back to Red{nbsp}Hat.
====
