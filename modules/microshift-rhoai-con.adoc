= How {rhoai-full} works in {microshift-short}

Edge deployments are where data happens and decisions need to be made. You can use {rhoai} to integrate a fleet of {microshift-short}-driven edge devices into the artificial intelligence and machine learning (AI/ML) operations cycle.

{rhoai-full} ({rhoia}) is a platform for data scientists and developers of AI/ML applications. Develop, train, and test an AI model in the cloud or data center using {rhoai}, then run your model in your edge deployments on {microshift-short}. Once you deploy your model, application data can be sent to the model. In turn, the model learns to make highly accurate predictions. Over time, your model can make data-driven decisions without a human user. This is an ideal scenario for edge applications.
