= OpenShift requirements 

[role="_abstract"]

Hardware and software requirements for {ols-long}, including supported {ocp-product-title} versions and CPU architectures.

{ocp-product-title} clusters enable telemetry by default.

* When telemetry is on, {ols-long} sends your chats and feedback to Red{nbsp}Hat.

* When telemetry is off, {ols-long} does not send this data.

* To stop {ols-long} from sending your chats and feedback, you must disable telemetry for the whole cluster.
