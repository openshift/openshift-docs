= Checklist for disabling network policy management in {SMProduct} 2

It is recommended that you:

* Disable network policy management in the {SMProduct} 2 `ServiceMeshControlPlane` resource.
* Complete the rest of the checklists.
* Migrate you deployment and workloads.
* Recreate your network policies after you have migrated your workloads.

However, if you cannot recreate your network policies after migrating for security reasons, then it is recommended that you:

* Recreate your network policies.
* Complete the rest of the checklists.
* Migrate your deployment and workloads.
