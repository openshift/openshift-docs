= System health dashboard page overview

The *System Health* dashboard page organizes information into the following groups:

Cluster status:: The overall status of the cluster, and the status of essential {rh-rhacs-first} components such as Sensor, Collector and Admission Controller.
Sensor upgrade:: The status of any pending or in-progress upgrades for Sensor.
Credential expiration:: The expiration dates of critical credentials. 
StackRox Scanner Vulnerability Definitions:: The status of the vulnerability definitions database that StackRox Scanner uses. 
Scanner V4 Vulnerabilities:: The vulnerability information associated with Scanner V4.
Image Integrations:: The health and connectivity status of integrated image registries. 
Notifier Integrations:: The health of any notifiers that you have integrated.
Backup Integrations:: The health of any backup providers that you have integrated.
Declarative configuration:: The status and consistency of the declarative configurations.
Central certificate:: The health and expiration status of the Central certificate.
StackRox Scanner certificate:: The health and expiration status of the StackRox Scanner certificate.
Scanner V4 certificate:: The health and expiration status of the Scanner V4 certificate.
