= Creating a `NonAdminBackup` CR

As a non-admin user, you can create a `NonAdminBackup` (NAB) CR to back up your application from your authorized namespace. A NAB CR is an OpenShift custom resource that securely facilitates the creation of a `Velero` backup object. The NAB CR reports back the status of the `Velero` backup by updating the `status.phase` field.
