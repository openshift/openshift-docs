= Compliance dashboard overview

[role="_abstract"]
You can check compliance status, view results, and generate reports by using the compliance dashboard in {product-title-short}.

By running compliance scans, you can check the compliance status of your entire infrastructure in {product-title-short}. You can view the results in the compliance dashboard, where you can filter data and monitor compliance status across clusters, namespaces, and nodes.

You can generate detailed compliance reports that focus on specific standards, controls, and industry benchmarks. Use these reports to track and share the compliance status of your environment. This ensures that your infrastructure meets the required compliance standards.
