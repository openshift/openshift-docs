= Sizing your Loki log store
Loki components are automatically sized based on your selection of the following:

* extra-small - One Loki cluster with one replica per component

* small - 500 GB/day 25-50 QPS at 200ms

* medium - 2 TB/day 25-75 QPS at 200ms
