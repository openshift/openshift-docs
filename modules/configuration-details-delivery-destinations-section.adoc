= Delivery destinations section

[role="_abstract"]
The *Delivery destinations* section displays information about email notification settings for compliance reports.

The *Delivery destinations* section has the following groups:

Email notifier:: Specifies the email notification system or tool set up to distribute reports or alerts.
Distribution list:: Lists the recipients to receive the notifications or reports.
Email template:: Specifies the email format for the notifications. You can use the default or customize the email subject and body as needed.
