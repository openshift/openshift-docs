= Updating the {clo}

To update the {clo} to a new minor release version, modify the update channel for the Operator subscription.

.Prerequisites

* You have installed {clo} v6.
* You have administrator permissions.
* You have access to the {product-title} web console.

.Procedure

. Navigate to *Operators* -> *Installed Operators*.

. Select the *openshift-logging* project.

. Click the *Red Hat OpenShift Logging* Operator.

. Click *Subscription*.

. In the *Subscription details* section, click the *Update channel* link.

. In the *Change Subscription Update Channel* window, select the update channel, *stable-{logging-major-version}.{logging-minor-version}*.

. Click *Save*. 

.Verification

. On the *Operators* > *Installed Operators* page, verify that the {clo} version matches `cluster-logging.{logging-major-version}{logging-minor-version}`.
  
. Wait for the *Status* field to report *Succeeded*.
