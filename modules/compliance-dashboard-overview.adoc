= Compliance dashboard overview

After you have performed a compliance scan, the compliance dashboard displays the results as the compliance status for your environment. You can view compliance violations directly from the dashboard, filter the detailed view and drill down into the compliance standards to find out if your environment is compliant against specific benchmarks. 

You can use shortcuts to check the compliance status of clusters, namespaces and nodes. Look for these shortcuts on the top of your compliance dashboard. By clicking on these shortcuts, you can view the compliance snapshot and generate reports on the overall compliance of your clusters, namespaces or nodes.
