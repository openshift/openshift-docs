= Compliance dashboard overview

[role="_abstract"]
The compliance dashboard displays compliance scan results and violations, allowing you to filter by benchmarks and drill down into compliance standards for clusters, namespaces, and nodes.

After you have performed a compliance scan, the compliance dashboard displays the results as the compliance status for your environment. You can view compliance violations directly from the dashboard. To discover if your environment is compliant against specific benchmarks, filter the detailed view and drill down into the compliance standards.

You can use shortcuts at the upper right of your compliance dashboard to check the compliance status of clusters, namespaces, and nodes. Click these shortcuts to view the compliance snapshot and generate reports on the overall compliance of your clusters, namespaces, or nodes.
