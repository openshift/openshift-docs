= Roles required for {GCP} Marketplace billing

If you are using {GCP} Marketplace-based billing to deploy a {product-title} cluster, you must prepare your {GCP} account by accepting the {GCP} terms and agreements.

To automate the checking and acceptance of these terms and agreements during {product-title} cluster creation, you must grant the `Consumer Procurement Entitlement Manager` role to the {GCP} identity (user or service account) that is creating the cluster.

The `Consumer Procurement Entitlement Manager` role includes the necessary permissions to check for existing consent to the {GCP} terms and agreements and grants consent if that has not yet been given.
