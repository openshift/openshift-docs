= Viewing the compliance status for a specific control

You can view the compliance status for a specific control for a selected standard.

.Procedure

. In the {product-title-short} portal, go to *Compliance -> Dashboard*.
. Look for the *Passing standards by cluster* widget.
. Click on a standard to view information about all the controls associated with that standard.
. From the *Controls* table, click on a control. A side panel opens on the right.
. In the side panel, click on the name of the control to view its details.
