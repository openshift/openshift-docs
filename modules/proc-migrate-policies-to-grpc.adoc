= Migrating policies to `GRPCRoute` support

:FeatureName: `GRPCRoute` policy attachment support

include::snippets/technology-preview.adoc[]

[role="_abstract"]
When you want to replace your `HTTPRoute` custom resources (CRs) with `GRPCRoute` CRs, you must update your policies to reference a `GRPCRoute` CR, and configure other parameters in those policies as required for your use case.

.Prerequisites

* You installed {ocp}.
* You installed {oc-first}.
* You installed the {mcpg} Operator.
* You created a Gateway object.
* You created a `GRPCRoute` CR.

.Procedure

. Update the `spec.targetRef.kind` parameter value in your `AuthPolicy`, `RateLimitPolicy`, and `PlanPolicy` CRs to `GRPCRoute` by using the following example:
+
.Example `AuthPolicy` CR updated to gRPC
[source,yaml]
----
#...
spec:
  targetRef:
    group: gateway.networking.k8s.io
    kind: GRPCRoute
    name: grpcstore
#...
----

. Update `PlanPolicy` CRs that reference HTTP-specific paths to use the well-known attributes, `request.grpc.service` and `request.grpc.method`.
+
.Example YAML block with gRPC traffic only
[source,yaml]
----
#...
when:
  - predicate: request.grpc.service == 'UserService'
  - predicate: request.grpc.method == 'GetUser'
#...
----
+
.Example YAML block for mixed traffic
[source,yaml]
----
#...
when:
  - predicate: request.headers['content-type'].startsWith('application/grpc')
  - predicate: request.grpc.service == 'UserService'
#...
----
+
If your `PlanPolicy` CR targets a specific `GRPCRoute` CR and not a `Gateway` object, you do not need the protocol check since only gRPC traffic flows through gRPC routes.

. Update your `PlanPolicy` CRs as needed with changes to `method` names and other details specific to your use case.

. Delete your outdated `HTTPRoute` CRs.
