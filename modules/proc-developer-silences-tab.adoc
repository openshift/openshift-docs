= Creating and managing silences from the Developer perspective

[role="_abstract"]
You can create and manage alert silences in the Developer perspective. Silences temporarily mute alerts that match specific label criteria, allowing you to suppress notifications while investigating or resolving issues.

.Prerequisites

* You have access to the cluster as a developer or as a user.
* You have the `cluster-monitoring-view` cluster role (cluster-wide scope).
* You have the `monitoring-alertmanager-edit` role for your namespace (project-scoped).
+
[NOTE]
====
To verify you have the required roles, run:

* `oc describe rolebinding -n <your-project> | grep monitoring-alertmanager-edit`
* `oc describe clusterrolebinding | grep cluster-monitoring-view`

If you do not have these roles, contact your cluster administrator to request them.
====
* Active or pending alerts exist in your project.

.Procedure

To create a silence from the Silences tab:

. In the Developer perspective of the {product-title} web console, click *Observe* and go to the *Silences* tab.

. Click *Create silence*.

. On the *Create silence* page, configure the silence:
.. In the *Schedule* section, set the start time and duration for the silence.
.. In the *Matchers* section, define label matchers to identify which alerts to silence:
+
--
* *Name*: The label name (for example, `alertname` or `severity`)
* *Value*: The label value to match
* *Operator*: Select whether the label must match exactly or use a regular expression
--
+
Add multiple matchers to create more specific silence rules. Alerts must match all label matchers to be silenced.
.. In the *Creator* field, optionally enter your name or identifier.
.. In the *Comment* field, add a comment explaining why the silence was created. A comment is required before saving.

. Click *Silence* to create the silence.

To create a silence for a specific alert from the Alerts tab:

. In the Developer perspective, click *Observe* and go to the *Alerts* tab.

. Find the alert you want to silence.

. Click {kebab} next to the alert and select *Silence alert*.
+
The *Create silence* page opens with pre-configured label matchers for the selected alert.

. Optional: Modify the silence configuration, such as duration or matchers.

. Add a comment explaining the reason for the silence.

. Click *Silence* to create the silence.

To view active and expired silences:

. In the Developer perspective, click *Observe* and go to the *Silences* tab.

. View the list of silences for your project.
+
The silences list displays:
+
--
* *Name*: Label matchers that define the silence
* *State*: Active, pending, or expired
* *Creator*: Who created the silence
* *Comment*: The reason for creating the silence
--

. Click a silence name to view its details, including start time, end time, and matched label criteria.

To edit or expire an active silence:

. In the Silences tab, find the silence you want to modify.

. Click {kebab} next to the silence and select one of the following:
+
--
* *Edit silence*: Modify the silence configuration, such as extending the duration or changing label matchers.
* *Expire silence*: Immediately end the silence and resume alert notifications.
--

.Verification

* The Silences tab displays the newly created silence in the Active state.
* Alerts matching the silence label criteria no longer trigger notifications while the silence is active.
