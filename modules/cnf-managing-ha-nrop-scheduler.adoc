= Managing high availability (HA) for the NUMA-aware scheduler

The numaresources-operator manages the High Availability (HA) of the topology-aware secondary scheduler based on how the spec.replicas field is configured in the NUMAResourcesScheduler Custom Resource (CR). As an OpenShift Administrator, you have three options for controlling the scheduler's behavior.
