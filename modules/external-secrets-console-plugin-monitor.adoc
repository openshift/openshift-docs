= Monitoring secrets management Operators by using {external-secrets-console-plugin}

[role="_abstract"]
You can monitor certificates, issuers, external secrets and secret stores of the installed secret management Operators across your clusters.

:FeatureName: {external-secrets-console-plugin}
include::snippets/technology-preview.adoc[]

.Prerequisites

* You have installed the {external-secrets-console-plugin}.

* You have installed at least one secret management Operator.

.Procedure

. Log in to the {product-title} web console.

. Navigate to *Plugins* -> *Secrets Management*.

. Click {kebab} for the custom resource that you want to monitor, and then click *Inspect*.

.Verification

. You must able to view the following sections:

** *Metadata*

** *Labels*

** *Annotation*

** *Specification*

** *Status*

** *Events*
