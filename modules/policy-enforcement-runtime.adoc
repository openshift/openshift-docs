= Runtime enforcement 

[role="_abstract"]
You can configure policies that are enforced during runtime to stop a pod that violates the policy or, in the case of audit log inspection, tell you of events that violate the policy but have already occurred and the system logged.

When enforced, a policy violation results in one or more of the following actions:

* Shutting down an offending pod, which results in creation of another healthy pod in its place
* {product-title-short} intercepting and preventing certain Kubernetes API calls 

[IMPORTANT]
====
When an image or workload rule triggers a violation, {product-title-short} generates a violation alert, but does _not_ disrupt the pod.
