= Expanding a {product-title} physical network to include new nodes

[role="_abstract"]
You can add {product-title} worker nodes to an existing physical network.

Expanding a physical network creates a new configuration under the same logical physical network.

.Procedure

. From the *Virtualization* perspective, click *Physical networks*.

. Click the three dots to the right of the network that you want to edit.

. Click *Configure nodes*. The *Network configuration wizard* is displayed.
+
[NOTE]
====
The **Physical network name** is predefined and cannot be edited during this process.
====

. Proceed through the wizard and add your additional nodes on the *Nodes configuration* page.
