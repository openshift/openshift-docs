= Intel Gaudi AI accelerators

:context: gaudi-ai-accelerator

:FeatureName: Intel Gaudi AI accelerators

{product-title} supports integration of Intel Gaudi AI accelerators for your generative AI and machine learning (AI/ML) applications. Intel Gaudi AI accelerators offer a cost-efficient, flexible, and scalable solution for optimized deep learning workloads.

Gaudi architecture includes integrated RDMA over Converged Ethernet (RoCE v2) that allow you to use a consistent scaling technology inside the server and rack and across racks. 

Red{nbsp}Hat supports Intel Gaudi devices up to Intel Gaudi 3. Gaudi 3 devices providing significant improvements in training speed and energy efficiency.

For more information, see link:https://docs.redhat.com/en/documentation/red_hat_openshift_ai_self-managed/2.20/html/working_with_accelerators/intel-gaudi-ai-accelerator-integration_accelerators#intel-gaudi-ai-accelerator-integration_accelerators
