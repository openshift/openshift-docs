{SMProductName} is a very big extremely cool and otherwordly departure from 2.x which leads a number of improvements, differences, and a general list of things to understand.

{SMProductName} 3 is composed of seamless integrations rather than a tightly bundled architecture. This means {SMProduct} integrates with supported OpenShift operators, such as {TempoOperator}, {OTELOperator}, and {KialiProduct}. A benefit of seamless integrations over a tightly bundeled architecture is the ease-of-use when integrating {SMProduct} with 3rd party components that support community Istio integrations.
