= Configuring Red{nbsp}Hat build of Karpenter
:context: rosa-nodes-autonode-configuring

toc::[]

[role="_abstract"]
After enabling {autonode} on your cluster, you create NodePools to define the compute capacity limits and instance requirements that {autonode} uses when provisioning nodes for your workloads.

NodePools specify allowed instance types, capacity types, such as on-demand or spot, availability zones, and resource limits. You can create multiple NodePools for different workload types with different requirements and priorities.

.Prerequisites

* You have enabled {autonode} on your cluster.
* You have verified that the `ec2nodeclass` and `openshiftec2nodeclass` resources are present and ready.
* You are logged in to your cluster as a user with `cluster-admin` privileges.
