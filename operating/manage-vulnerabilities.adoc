[role="_abstract"]
Security vulnerabilities in your environment may be exploited by an attacker to perform unauthorized actions such as denial of service, remote code execution, or unauthorized access to sensitive data.
Therefore, the management of vulnerabilities is a foundational step towards a successful Kubernetes security program.
