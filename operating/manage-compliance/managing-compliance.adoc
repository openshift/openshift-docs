= Managing the compliance feature
include::modules/common-attributes.adoc[]
:context: managing-compliance

toc::[]

You can view the compliance results associated with your cluster by using the compliance feature in the {rh-rhacs-first} portal. The feature summarizes the compliance information collected by the Compliance Operator in a single interface.

//Compliance assessment and reporting by using RHACS
include::modules/compliance-assessment-and-reporting-by-using-rhacs.adoc[leveloffset=+1]

By performing a compliance scan in {product-title-short}, you can monitor your entire infrastructure and ensure that it meets the required standards, and use the compliance dashboard for filtering and detailed reporting.

For more information, see xref:../../operating/manage-compliance/performing-and-monitoring-compliance-scans.adoc#performing-and-monitoring-compliance-scans[Performing and monitoring compliance scans].

By creating and managing compliance scan schedules on the schedules page, you can monitor the compliance status across all the clusters and ensure that no duplicate profile scans are performed in a cluster.

For more information, see xref:../../operating/manage-compliance/creating-and-managing-compliance-scan-schedules.adoc#creating-and-managing-compliance-scan-schedules[Creating and managing compliance scan schedules].
